# Privacy Policy

**English** | [한국어](PRIVACY_POLICY.ko.md)

**Effective Date: March 26, 2026**

## Introduction

DiveChecker ("the App") is a freediving equalization pressure monitoring application developed by KodeNet ("we", "us", "our"). This Privacy Policy explains how the App handles user information.

## Data Collection

**The App does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data.**

All data generated by the App — including pressure measurement sessions, chart annotations, and user preferences — is stored **exclusively on your device** in local storage (SQLite database and SharedPreferences). No data is sent to external servers, cloud services, or third parties.

## Network Access

The App does **not** require internet access. The release build does not include the `INTERNET` permission. The App functions entirely offline.

## Hardware Communication

The App communicates with the DiveChecker hardware device via **USB MIDI** connection only. This communication occurs locally between your device and the connected hardware. No data from this communication is transmitted over any network.

## Device Authentication

The App uses ECDSA P-256 cryptographic signature verification to authenticate connected DiveChecker hardware devices. This process is performed locally on-device and does not involve any external servers.

## Third-Party Services

The App does **not** integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, crash reporting, or tracking services. There are no third-party SDKs that collect user data.

## Data Storage and Retention

- **Measurement data**: Stored locally in SQLite database on your device
- **User preferences**: Stored locally via SharedPreferences (theme, language, notification settings, etc.)
- **Data export**: You may manually export measurement data as JSON files to your device's storage. This is entirely user-initiated and remains on your device.

You can delete all app data at any time by clearing the app's data through your device settings or by uninstalling the App.

## Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under the age of 13. It is a specialized tool designed for freediving training that requires dedicated hardware equipment.
